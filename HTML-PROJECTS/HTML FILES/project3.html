Coalation Goverenment

Why in the news?

Recently concluded 2024 General Elections of Lok Sabha resulted in formation of a coalition government at the centre as no political party got a clear majority in the Lower House of the Parliament.

About Coalation Goverenment
  • Refers to a political arrangement where multiple parties collaborate to form a government when no single party secures a clear majority
  • Factors contributing for Coalition Government in India:
    • Multi-party system
    • Regional diversity
    • state parties,
    • anti-incumbency factors
Challenges due to Coalition Government
  • Political instability
  • Policy paralysis
  • Myopic Decision Making
  • Compromise on ideologies
